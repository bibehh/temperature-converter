Temperature conversion refers to the process of converting a temperature reading from one unit of measurement to another. The most common temperature scales are Celsius (°C), Fahrenheit (°F), and Kelvin (K). Here are the formulas for converting between these scales:

1. Celsius to Fahrenheit:

F = (9/5 * C) + 32

Where C is the temperature in Celsius and F is the temperature in Fahrenheit.

2. Fahrenheit to Celsius:

C = 5/9 * (F - 32)

Where F is the temperature in Fahrenheit C is the temperature in Celsius.
